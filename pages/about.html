How This Was Built

Editorial Standards

01
Primary sources only for factual claims. Every deal term, financial figure, and biographical fact is sourced to a specific SEC filing on EDGAR, an official company investor relations press release, or a verifiable public corporate document. Secondary or aggregated sources are used only to corroborate, not as primary references.
02
Inline citation for all deal-specific facts. SEC EDGAR filing links, IR page URLs, and press release dates are included immediately adjacent to the facts they support, not relegated to footnotes.
03
Pending transactions clearly labeled. The TopBuild acquisition has been announced but not closed as of April 25, 2026. All references to post-close positioning are explicitly labeled as pending and subject to shareholder vote and regulatory clearance.
04
Performance figures attributed to their original sources. Claims about stock performance (50-bagger, 200-bagger, S&P outperformance) are attributed to Jacobs Private Equity's official biography and Bloomberg market data as cited in official materials, not independently calculated by the author.
05
Illustrative charts labeled as such. EBITDA multiple ranges on the Roll-Up Playbook page are illustrative, based on industry convention and publicly stated transaction multiples, not proprietary analysis. They are labeled accordingly.
06
Stock prices are delayed market data. The Dashboard pulls from Yahoo Finance's public endpoint and may reflect prices delayed by 15-20 minutes. This is not a financial data service and should not be used for trading decisions.
